Featured Trails

The Shenandoah Valley offers trails for every type of hiker, from easy, scenic walks through wooded glades to demanding climbs up rocky summits. Whether you’re exploring peaceful streams or chasing stunning mountain views, there’s a trail to match your adventure.

Old Rag Mountain

Known for its challenging climb and stunning views.

Difficulty: Hard

Distance and Duration: ~9 miles; 5-7 hours

Old Rag Mountain

Stony Man Trail

Short, easy hike with beautiful views.

Difficulty: Easy

Distance and Duration: 1.6 miles; ~1 hour

Stony Man Trail

Dark Hollow Falls Trail

A popular trail leading to a cascading waterfall.

Difficulty: Moderate

Distance and Duration: 1.4 miles; ~1-2 hours

Dark Hollow Falls Trail

Hawksbill Summit

The highest peak in Shenandoah National Park, offering panoramic vistas.

Difficulty: Moderate

Distance and Duration: 1.5-2.9 miles; 1-3 hours

Hawksbill Summit Trail
