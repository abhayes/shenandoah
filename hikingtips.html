Hiking Tips

Essential Tips for a Safe and Responsible Adventure in the Shenandoah Valley

Preparation

Websites: Shenandoah National Park site, AllTrails App

Water, snacks, map/compass, sunscreen, first aid kit, flashlight.

Comfortable hiking boots, weather-appropriate clothing.

The Shenandoah Valley experiences distinct seasonal weather patterns, making each season unique for hiking, so plan your hike based on your weather preferences and the experiences you're seeking.

Safety Tips

Stay on marked trails

Hike with a buddy

Be aware of wildlife

Avoid overexertion

Leave No Trace Principles

  1. Plan Ahead and Prepare
  2. Travel and Camp on Durable Surfaces
  3. Dispose of Waste Properly
  4. Leave What You Find
  5. Minimize Campfire Impact
  6. Respect Wildlife
  7. Be Considerate of Other Visitors
